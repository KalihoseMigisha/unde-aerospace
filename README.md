# UNDE AEROSPACE

> **Autonomy for the African Horizon.**

Unde Aerospace is developing the intelligent flight layer for the next generation of African logistics. By integrating high-performance **TPU-trained neural networks** with industry-standard flight stacks, we are building autonomous systems capable of navigating the unique challenges of the African landscape.

## 🛸 The Vision
We don't just fly; we perceive. Unde Aerospace leverages **JAX** and **Keras** to provide real-time, sub-second reasoning at the edge. We stand on the "Wings of Giants," utilizing **ArduPilot** and **PX4** for mission-critical stability while focusing our innovation on high-level autonomous decision-making.

## 🛠 Tech Stack
* **Intelligence:** JAX / Keras (Private Weights)
* **Flight Foundation:** ArduPilot / MAVLink
* **Compute:** TPU-accelerated training / Edge-optimized inference
* **Environment:** Managed via `uv` for reproducible aerospace engineering.

## 🌍 Impact
Designed in Tanzania. Built for the Continent. Our mission is to provide affordable, locally-governed drone infrastructure for agriculture, medical delivery, and rural connectivity.
